High-Access Execution

Utilizing SPRAT/IRATA certified rope access methodologies, we reach critical elevations safely and rapidly. We bypass the logistical nightmares of swing stages and scaffolding to execute direct-contact inspections and repairs.

Led by a Certified Thermographer, our teams deploy FLIR C8 imaging hardware directly on the wall. We detect invisible moisture pockets, failing gaskets, and compromised sealants behind the substrate before they cause structural failure.

From targeted sealant replacement to full-scale architectural wall restorations, we apply industry-leading materials to seal the building envelope, stopping active leaks and extending the lifespan of your property.
